$$$d_f$$$ - Diameter of Fitments

The diameter of the fitment bars which make up the transvers reinforcement. (The ribcage of the beam)

These bars are used to hold longitudinal bars into a cage structure during construction.

These bars are used to provide shear reinforcement.
