$$$L_{ef}$$$ - Effective Span

Length of effective span of a beam or slab.

AS3600 1.7 For a beam or slab taken as the lesser of $$$(L_n + D)$$$ and $$$L$$$ for a beam or slab
For a cantilever $$$Ln + D/2$$$

Related:

$$$L_n$$$ Clear Span

$$$L$$$ Centre-to-centre distance between the supports of a flexural member

$$$\Delta/L_{ef}$$$ Deflection Limit. See AS3600 2.3.2.

Deemed to comply span-to-depth ratio for beams and slabs. See AS3600 8.5.4 and 9.3.4.1 respectively.
